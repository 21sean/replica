Build and refine your app with the Agent

Start by telling the Agent what kind of app or site you want to build. Then keep improving it by chatting. The Agent reads its own files, applies your feedback, and serves the result instantly in the live preview.

For heavier tasks, switch to a bigger model or turn on thinking mode. It is your hardware, so it is your call.

Turn on thinking models for more complex tasks

Point Replica at any reasoning-capable model in your Ollama library. Watch the thought process stream in as it plans your app, or collapse it and just see results.

Everything built in: editor, preview, console, and hosting

Every project comes with a real file workspace, a live preview pane, and a console for running scripts. No setup, no config files, no waiting. It works the moment the Agent writes it.

Ask again and the Agent refines its own work

Change your mind as often as you like. The Agent edits the exact files it wrote instead of starting over, so your project evolves turn by turn until it feels right.
